Since When Did I Become a K-pop Fan?

┏━✦❘༻༺❘✦━━┓

One of the most popular music genres these days is K-pop. Numerous K-pop band idols have achieved success and turned into the biggest brand ambassadors.

I won't deny that K-pop's dances, songs, and visuals are its main draws. Since 2014, I have been a K-pop fan.

It all began when my sister purchased a t-shirt bearing the name Sehun, a member of the EXO group. So, from there I knew the existence of K-pop and started to be interested in some groups.

Among the earliest K-pop groups that I was interested in were Bigbang, 2ne1, Exo, Gfriend and EXID.

Actually now, I am a fan of a lot of K-pop groups, like BTS, NCT, BlackPink, Riize, Enhypen, Aespa, Babymonster etc.

RIIZE is a rookie boy group that debuted under SM Entertainment in September 2023.

Their fandom is known as BRIIZE. Actually, I was a RIIZE fan during their pre-debut, which was before they made their debut.

They are well-known because they been active during their pre-debut.

The reason I became BRIIZE was because of the concept song they released, which described their group as being similar to K-POP in the 1990s.

So, for me, it's unique, and their songs are enjoyable to listen too. I should be interested in all of their members, but Wonbin has piqued my interest the most.

My favourite songs by RIIZE are 'Memories' and 'Love 119'.
